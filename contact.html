Authenticity

Key rotation & trust surface.

Keep verification simple and predictable so people can trust that they are speaking to the right node.

Canonical source: This page is the primary reference for active email, Nostr keys, and PGP fingerprints. If a contact detail is not listed here, treat it as untrusted by default.

Rotation: Any key or address change will be announced on this page and signed with the previous valid PGP key wherever possible. Ignore unsolicited “new key” messages that are not backed by this page and a valid signature.

Out-of-band verification: For high-stakes coordination, verify fingerprints via a second channel (voice, video, or a known-trust intermediary) before sending sensitive data.

No surprise accounts: There are no “secret” social media accounts, custodial wallets, or random chat handles. If something matters, it will route through channels listed here or through explicit, verifiable extensions of them.
